## OEM Hardware Breakouts

Different OEM / hardware vendors, have a range of different keyboard shortcuts that can be used to launch applications or perform actions. 

The Lenovo shortcuts listed below have been tested, although the shortcut to launch MS Teams only works on some generations of Lenovo business series hosts. The Dell shortcuts were vibe suggested by our AI friends, but have not been validated yet (did not encounter any Dell Kiosks to try these on as of the time of writing).

## Test Cases

Lenovo Think series business devices:

 - Ctrl+Alt+Win+Shift+F12 (Launch MS Teams)
 - F12 (Sometimes also launches MS Teams)
 - Ctrl+Alt+Win+Shift+Esc (works like Alt Tab to change window focus)

Dell devices:

Try Function key and F1-F12 to see if any unexpected host interaction occurs. 

## Next Steps

Launching MS Teams provides possible pathways to a File Open dialog box, as well as an opportunity to smuggle a payload (LotL binary, beacon, or RMM) onto the host.  

## Ethical Note

Remember to only test on your own kiosks, or with permission such as in the scope of a bug bounty or penetration test, with good intentions of helping elevate kiosk security.
