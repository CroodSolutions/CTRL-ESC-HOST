## Introduction

This section provides a range of tactics that you can try for getting initial kiosk breakout from 3rd Party Windows Kiosks. This is where you will get your first dialog box or starting point, after which, there are links and references to the later sections describing what to do / test-for next.  Keyboard shortcuts and approaches are broken out into individual articles, with flow paths outlined to take you to the appropriate next steps following the initial escape path.

Remember to only test on your own kiosks, or with permission such as in the scope of a bug bounty or penetration test, with good intentions of helping elevate kiosk security.
