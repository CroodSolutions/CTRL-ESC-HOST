## What This Is

CTRL+ESC+HOST is a collection of atomic tests, workflows, and writeups to help you identify and validate escape-to-host flaws, with a focus on kiosks and presented applications.

## Start Here

- [Introduction]({{ "/introduction/" | relative_url }}): What escape-to-host is, why it matters, and how to use this framework.
- [Kiosk Playbook]({{ "/kiosk-playbook/" | relative_url }}): Platform-specific workflows (Windows, Chrome, Linux, etc.).
- [Presented App Playbook]({{ "/presented-app-playbook/" | relative_url }}): Guidance for Citrix/VMware and other presentation layers.
- [Specific Escape Scenarios]({{ "/escape-scenarios/" | relative_url }}): End-to-end scenarios and real-world walkthroughs.
- [Defensive Recommendations]({{ "/defensive-recommendations/" | relative_url }}): Hardening and mitigations.

## Use Responsibly

Only test systems you own or have explicit written permission to assess.
